ben is a professional sound designer from Alberta, Canada. having worked on titles such as Need for Speed and Shotgun Farmers, ben studied sound design for visual media at the Vancouver Film School and has been helping triple-A and independent game development teams bring a voice to their vision.
If not at his desk with his headphones on, you can find ben venturing out with microphone in hand or experimenting with new ways to capture interesting sounds.
